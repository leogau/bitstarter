FAQ

How will it know what to find?
Your Minion needs to learn what objects you want to find by taking pictures of the object. Then, using voice activation, the Minion sets off scanning objects around your house. When it finds what you're looking for, it'll play a sound until you get there.
How will it be able to move around?
Minions are small spherical robots which will be able to rolls around on the floor.
How can I keep track of where my Minion is?
There is an accompanying smartphone app which you can use to:
  • See out of the camera equipped on the Minion
  • Make the Minion play a sound
  • Tell how close you are to the Minion
